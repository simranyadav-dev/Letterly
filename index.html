Getting Started

Welcome to Pixel Typing Master! This guide will help you understand all the features and get the most out of your typing practice.

Game Setup

  • Choose your time duration from the dropdown (1-30 minutes)
  • Select your difficulty level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert)
  • Click "START GAME" to begin your typing session
  • The timer will start counting down immediately

Difficulty Levels

  • Beginner: Simple sentences with basic vocabulary and punctuation
  • Intermediate: Programming and technical content with moderate complexity
  • Advanced: Complex technical topics with challenging vocabulary
  • Expert: Advanced concepts with sophisticated terminology

Typing Practice

  • Type the displayed text exactly as shown
  • Green highlighting indicates correct characters
  • Red highlighting shows typing errors
  • The current position is highlighted in the text
  • New content automatically loads when you finish a paragraph

Tips for Better Typing

  • Focus on accuracy first, speed will develop naturally
  • Use proper finger placement on the home row keys
  • Keep your wrists straight and fingers curved
  • Look at the screen, not the keyboard
  • Practice regularly for consistent improvement

Results and Statistics

After completing your session, you'll see detailed statistics including your Words Per Minute (WPM), accuracy percentage, total characters typed, and error count. Use these metrics to track your progress and identify areas for improvement.
